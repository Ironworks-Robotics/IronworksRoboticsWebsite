About Us

We are Ironworks Robotics, a FIRST Robotics team based in Richmond Hill, Ontatio from Alexander Mackenzie High School. We are a diverse and welcoming community filled with students from all walks of life each with unique interests and skills. Our team is made of students from all grades, including students from the International Baccalaureate and Arts Program

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire students to develop a passion for learning and a passion for STEM disciplines. Our ultimate goal is to build a successful team and community here in our school while in the process, learning and developing skills from technical aptitude to leadership, cooperation, and responsibility.
